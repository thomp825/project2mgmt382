Meet the Founders

Christie and Chris are passionate chefs with years of experience.

Twenty years ago, Christie and Chris embarked on a culinary journey fueled by a deep-seated passion for cooking and a desire to share their family's rich culinary heritage with others. Drawing inspiration from cherished family recipes passed down through generations, they envisioned a cozy retreat where food enthusiasts could gather, learn, and indulge in the joy of cooking. Thus, Christie and Chris’s Cooking Cabin was born—an inviting haven where the aroma of home-cooked meals and the warmth of shared experiences blend seamlessly to create unforgettable culinary adventures.
